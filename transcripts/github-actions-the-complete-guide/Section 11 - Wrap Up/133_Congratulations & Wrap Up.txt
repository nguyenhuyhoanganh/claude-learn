# Congratulations & Wrap Up

Congratulations, you finished this course, and you learned a lot about GitHub Actions therefore. Now, my recommendation for you, even though it might sound obvious, is that you now take what you learned and you practice it. You practice it at some dummy projects, some demo projects which you build. You can take the course projects and build up on those, and build your own workflows. Try out different things, be creative and experiment because that's the best way of learning something and of mastering something. And of course, feel free to dive back into some course videos or sections, if something's not entirely clear yet, that's totally fine and the big advantage of a video on-demand course. But definitely practice, practice and practice because as with everything else in life practice makes perfect also when it comes to GitHub Actions.
