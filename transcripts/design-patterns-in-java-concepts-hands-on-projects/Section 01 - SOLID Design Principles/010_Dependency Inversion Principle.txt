# Dependency Inversion Principle

The dependency inversion principle. The last one from the Solid Design Principles Group. And the big one. And I know a lot of you are probably aware of this principle already. By the way, in case you don't know, this was published by Robert Martin. And let's look at the formal definition of this principle. Now, this principle is comes in two different parts. The first part states that high level modules should not depend on low level modules. Both should depend on abstraction. The second part of the principle tells us that abstractions should not depend on details. Rather, details should depend on abstractions.
