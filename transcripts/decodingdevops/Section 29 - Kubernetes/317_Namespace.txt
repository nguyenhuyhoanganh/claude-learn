Namespaces provides a mechanism for isolating groups of resources within a single cluster. Names of resources needs to be unique within a namespace. So let's say you have a pod named NGINX one, you cannot have one more pod with the same name, but in another namespace you can have pod with the same name. So once again, names of the resource needs to be unique within namespace, but not across namespaces. And when should you really use multiple namespaces? I have seen for small environments people don't even care about namespaces. They create most of the thing in default namespace.
