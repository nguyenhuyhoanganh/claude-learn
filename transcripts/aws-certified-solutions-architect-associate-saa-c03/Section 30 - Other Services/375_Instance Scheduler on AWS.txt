It's quite complete and quite complicated, but the idea is that you deploy this using CloudFormation and this provides you a capability in your account to automatically start and stop your AWS services in order to reduce costs, maybe up to 70%. For example, if you have a use case where you want to stop the company's EC2 instances outside business hours, then you may want to use the Instance Scheduler solution on AWS.
