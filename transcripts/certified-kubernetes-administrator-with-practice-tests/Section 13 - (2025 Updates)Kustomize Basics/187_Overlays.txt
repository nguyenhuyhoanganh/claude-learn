And so if we have a development environment and a staging environment and a production environment, we may wanna tweak certain properties on a per-environment basis, and that's where Kustomize really does come into play. So how exactly do we accomplish this? Well, it's with something called overlays. And if you take a look at a usual folder structure of a customized project, it's gonna be broken down into two different sections. So you're gonna first have the base section.
