# Module Introduction

Just as with Just Docker and Docker Compose, we don't just wanna have a look at how to start our containers, how to add volumes, but we also wanna make sure that they can communicate with each other and that we can reach all the containers that should be exposed to the outside world. Therefore, in this course section we are going to dive into Kubernetes &amp; Networking as I like to call it. We're going to have a look at how we can connect our pods and containers with each other and with the world. And we're going to start by taking another brief look at services something we already used over the last lectures on modules of course, but we're going to take another brief look here but then we're going to focus on a couple of new concepts. We're going to have a look at Pod-Internal Communication, which means we'll have a pod with more than one container inside and we wanna ensure that these containers can talk to each other and we're going to have a look at Pod-To-Pod Communication thereafter. And that's another thing we haven't considered up to this point. So let's dive in.
