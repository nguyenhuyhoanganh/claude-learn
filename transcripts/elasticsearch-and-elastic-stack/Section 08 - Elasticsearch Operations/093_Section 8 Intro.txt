# Section 8 Intro

Time to get real. It's all well and good to learn about elastic search on a single node, but in the real world, you'll have lots of operational concerns as you set up and maintain an entire cluster. That's what this next section is about. We'll cover how to make the right architectural choices about your cluster, how to choose the right hardware for it, and how to scale it as your application in your data grows. We'll also look at another part of the elastic stack, called X pack, which you can use to monitor your cluster, and we'll see how you can prepare for the worst by practicing how fail over works, how to take snapshots of your data for backups, and how to safely restart your cluster, when you need to update its software. There is a lot to cover here so let's get started.
